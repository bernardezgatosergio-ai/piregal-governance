    • Respect for Specification: Piregal will not specify alternative brands, and Herculan will not support other installers in the Spanish naval cluster [cite: 2026-02-18].
    • Compliance with Protocols: No shortcuts are allowed in the Operational Protocol, PSAH, NCR, or ITP. Compliance is mandatory without exceptions [cite: 2026-02-18].

    3. PROTECTION AND EXCELLENCE

    • Client Protection: We guarantee verifiable quality and assume real responsibility; the client is never left unprotected [cite: 2026-02-18].
    • Zero Tolerance for Negligence: Applying outside environmental conditions or failing to record data in the ITP is considered a serious breach and incompatible with Operational Symbiosis [cite: 2026-02-18].
    • Legal and Environmental Compliance: Absolute rigor with European and local regulations, including MARPOL waste management standards [cite: 2026-02-18].
