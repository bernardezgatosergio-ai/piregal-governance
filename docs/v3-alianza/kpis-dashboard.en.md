The PSAH cannot function without a robust measurement system. KPIs are the mechanism that turns strategy into data and decisions into a competitive advantage. These indicators measure what truly matters: speed, quality, specification, compliance, efficiency, and growth.

### 1. STRATEGIC KPIs (Management Level)
*Reviews: Quarterly and Bi-annual | Owners: Piregal + Herculan Management*
* **1.1. Herculan Specification Rate (Goal: 70%):** Herculan-specified projects / Total tendered projects.
* **1.2. Naval Market Share (Goal: NW Leadership):** Applied SL 500 sqm / Total cluster sqm.
* **1.3. Healthcare Penetration (Goal: 2 active groups):** Active healthcare projects / Tendered projects.
* **1.4. Joint Alliance Margin:** (Piregal Margin + Herculan Margin) / Closed projects.
* **1.5. Territorial Expansion (Goal: Mediterranean 2027):** New active territories / Target territories.

### 2. OPERATIONAL KPIs (Monthly PSAH Level)
*Reviews: Monthly | Owners: Site Management + Logistics + Quality*
* **2.1. Vigo Hub Response Time (Goal: <48h):** Hours from request to Intervention Kit delivery.
* **2.2. Critical Stockouts (Goal: 0%):** Stock incidents / Total requests.
* **2.3. Critical Window Compliance (Goal: 95%):** Windows executed without interruption / Scheduled windows.
* **2.4. Technical Incidents (Goal: 0%):** Application-related incidents / Executed projects.
* **2.5. NCR Resolution (Goal: 100%):** Closed NCRs / Issued NCRs.
* **2.6. Digital Traceability ITP (Goal: 100%):** Completed ITPs / Executed projects.

### 3. COMMERCIAL AND QUALITY KPIs
* **Tender Success Ratio:** Goal 40% in Naval, 30% in Civil.
* **Client Retention:** 100% repetition rate for strategic clients.
* **Warranty Failures (Goal: 0%):** Valid claims / Total executed projects.
* **Color Errors (Goal: <1%):** Detected deviations / Total mixes performed.

### 4. OFFICIAL PSAH DASHBOARD
The Dashboard is visual and organized into four control panels:
1.  **Operational Status:** Critical stock, response times, open NCRs, and ITPs.
2.  **Commercial Status:** Pipeline, win rate, and strategic clients.
3.  **Strategic Status:** Specification, market share, and expansion.
4.  **Quality Status:** Warranties, internal/external audits, and technical incidents.

### 5. INTERPRETATION RULES
* **Red:** Immediate corrective action required.
* **Yellow:** Mandatory review in the next session.
* **Green:** Standard maintained.
* **Triple Red:** Activation of an extraordinary PSAH review.
