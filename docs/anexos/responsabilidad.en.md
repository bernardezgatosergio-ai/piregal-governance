### 3. RESPONSIBILITY SCENARIOS
* **3.1. Environmental Failure:** If Piregal applies out of range, Piregal is liable. If the shipyard forces application, the shipyard is liable (documented via NOD).
* **3.2. Defective Product:** Primary Resp.: Herculan. Actions: Material replacement by Herculan and repair executed by Piregal.
* **3.3. Incorrect Application:** Primary Resp.: Piregal. Actions: Re-application at no cost and joint technical report.
* **3.4. Mixed Failure:** 50/50 joint responsibility. The alliance responds as a single technical organism.
